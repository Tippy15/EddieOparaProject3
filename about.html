Eddie Opara was born in
Wandsworth, London in 1972.
He grew up enjoying art, and
would draw things like 7-up and
Coca-Cola botles because the
colors excited him.

He studied Graphic Design at
the University of Arts London
from 1991-1995. He then
received a MFA from Yale after
his time there in 1995-1997.
