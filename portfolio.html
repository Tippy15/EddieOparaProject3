Branding - Coqual

Eddie aimed to avoid the typical marijuana
tropes and decided to do a protest graphics
approach to reflect the objectives of the brand
to right the wrongs of the War on Drugs.

A logo was created that consisted of three
circles and bars to create an abstract
monogram and is meant to portray
performance. It allows to logo to move, play
and dance like the performances seen on the
stages.

The objective of this branding was to create a
visual personality that was bold and demanded
to be seen. This was achieved with a custom
drawn wordmark and a condensed typography. New symbols were created as well to invoke a feeling of currency or factor of exchange.
