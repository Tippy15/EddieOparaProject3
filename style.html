Eddie Opara is a self proclaimed
generalist, as in he moves from
different mediums seamlessly.
When he begins projects, he
takes a look at the outside world
and essays and takes inspiration
when suggesting ideas to clients.

He once hated color
in design, but now he
sees there's no correct
way to perceive color.
He uses color to
contextualize the
topic in his projects.

He takes knowledge
in craftsmanship and
architecture,
especially in
eclecticism, when he
designs his projects.
